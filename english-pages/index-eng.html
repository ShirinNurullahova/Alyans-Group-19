WHO ARE WE?

How can our services help your business?

You do not have to spend extra to keep an accountant in your office. You do not buy equipment and office supplies to do your job. You do not need additional space for the employee. In an emergency, our team always has a "reserve player". You don't have to worry if your accountant goes on vacation, gets sick, or is completely out of work at the most important moment. Our reserve employee will always help you. You will entrust your financial affairs to us and manage your business. A package of private services will be prepared according to your requirements. We will treat you individually.

Who benefits from outsourcing accounting?

No matter what your company does, no matter how many employees you work for, you will reap financial benefits by handing over your accounting to AlyansGroup19.

Small entrepreneurs

We will help you reduce your accounting costs by 30% or more. We guarantee a fixed cost of our services, no more payments. We are building electronic document management. We pay attention to communication with supervisory authorities and accompany table inspections. We quickly and easily transfer your company from visual accounting to AlyansGroup19. We guarantee confidentiality.

Medium business

We choose a team of specialists and a personal manager for you. We conduct monthly preliminary calculation of taxes (forecasting the tax burden). We undertake the establishment and maintenance of management and production accounting. We offer full-time accounting support from primary organizations to the position of chief accountant. We accompany your transition to AlyansGroup19 according to the "road map".

Big business

We create specialized project teams taking into account the needs of your work. We are engaged in the establishment and maintenance of management and production accounting, preparation of corporate reports. We provide process automation and integration of accounting with accounting processes. We support interaction with regulatory authorities, tax audits and annual audits. We are ready to work in accordance with the rules and algorithms adopted by your company.
