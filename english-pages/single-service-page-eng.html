Mandatory audit

Mandatory audit is an annual scheduled independent audit of accounting and financial statements of business entities for the period of such audit in accordance with existing legislation.

• aaOfficial verification of the accuracy of the organization's accounting reports.

• aaAssess the quality of internal control, workflow and accounting organization.

• aaProviding auditor's opinion, comments and clarifications, reporting, etc. on all issues related to accounting and tax accounting.

• aaPreparation of recommendations on the results of the audit and preparation of the report.

Who needs it?

The following are subject to mandatory audit:

• aaState extra-budgetary and investment funds, credit and insurance organizations, as well as sources of formation of mutual funds, mutual insurance companies, stock (commodity) exchanges provided by the current legislation of the Russian Federation.

• aaFirms with assets of more than 60 million rubles at the end of the year preceding the reporting year.

• aaOpen joint stock companies, insurance, credit, investment organizations and humanitarian enterprises, regardless of their financial statements, as well as other organizations subject to mandatory audit by federal law.

• aaLegal entities with income of more than 400 million rubles from the sale of products, works or services to the organization for the year preceding the reporting year. In this case, cooperation with agricultural cooperatives and associations of these cooperatives is an exception.

What will I get?

Based on the results of the legal audit, the following is provided:

• aaRelevant auditor's report in which the auditor has the opportunity to express his or her professional opinion on the reliability of the financial statements submitted by the companies.

• aaAn audit report describing in detail all the comments and recommendations of the expert to eliminate errors and inaccuracies submitted in writing.

• aaResults of financial analysis on eight key performance indicators of your organization.
